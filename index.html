Flowsheet Routing

Flowsheet routing is the act of ensuring the connection between nodes on a flowsheet are orthogonal. For user-generated projects this could happen on project load and could trigger up to 8 hour GUI freezes. I moved the route calculations off the event dispatch thread (Swingâ€™s GUI thread), ensuring asynchronous execution. I also altered all routing actions so they would be undoable, parallelizable and cancellable.
